Organisation's history

Mashesha Flavor Haven started in 2022 as a small kitchen, today we have our own property and equipment which shows that we have grown over the years. Mashesha flavour Haven has been a resturant that aims to bring a unique flavour and experience of the local traditional African food.

We have been focusing on high quality freshly prepared dishes that cater to a varity of tastes. Our resturant is a go to destination for delicious and freshly prepared fast food.

Mission and Vision

Mission - Our mission is to bring together people of our community through African dishes made with love and tradition. We aim to create a warm and welcoming space where people enjoy food as every meal is a journey through Africa's culinary heritage

Vision - We plan to extend our business all over the country and make African food a beloved choice for people from all background while maintaining excellence in everything we do.
